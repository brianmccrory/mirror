All of Me is a classy and dependable jazz joint in rowdy Roppongi. Many live shows at All of Me feature a singer or several singers with a jazz band; the musicians are usually top-notch. The food is nice, restaurant-quality food, better than the average bar fare.

This bar is also famous for all-night live shows and jam sessions on selected Fridays and Saturdays, starting just after midnight until about 3:00 am. Check the schedule for details.
